# Understanding rates

Rates determine how strongly and how quickly your quad responds to stick movements. This page is prepared as a starting point for your own notes.

## The three important values

| Value | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Center Sensitivity | Response around the center of the stick |
| Max Rate | Maximum rotation speed at full stick deflection |
| Expo | Shapes the transition between center and full deflection |

!!! warning "Do not copy blindly"
    Rates are highly personal. Make gradual changes and test them in a safe environment.
