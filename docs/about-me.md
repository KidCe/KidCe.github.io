## My FPV journey

I started flying FPV in September 2023 and entered racing in early 2024. Since then, racing has become a major part of the hobby for me. I have competed at the major German Aircrasher events as well as the MultiGP European Championship.

In 2026, I am taking the next step and attending more international events, with the goal of racing at as many major European competitions as possible.
